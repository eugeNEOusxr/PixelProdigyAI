Powerful Features

Everything you need for professional 3D creation

🎯

Advanced Selection

Box, Circle, Lasso, and Paint selection tools. Grow, shrink, and invert selections with keyboard shortcuts. Precision vertex selection for detailed work.

🖌️

Sculpt Brushes

Draw, Smooth, Inflate, Grab, Pinch, Flatten, and Crease brushes. Adjustable radius, strength, and falloff curves. Symmetry modes for balanced sculpting.

📚

Layer System

Non-destructive multi-layer workflow. Increment/decrement vertices per layer. Layer isolation for focused editing. Capture frames as background images.

🔗

Object Binding

Connect objects with Rigid, Elastic, Chain, or Weld constraints. Color-coded tethers with adjustable strength. Physics-driven interactions.

💥

Fragmentation

Shatter objects with Voronoi, Voxel, Radial, or Slice algorithms. Configurable fragment count and scatter velocity. Physics-enabled fragments.

✨

Particle Effects

Smoke, Sparks, Embers, Debris, and Fire particles. GPU-accelerated with 10k capacity. Point, Area, and Trail emitter types.

🤖

AI Suggestions

Context-aware pattern recommendations. 12 intelligent procedural patterns. Organic Flow, Geometric, Crystalline, Erosion, and more. Zero backend required.

🌍

Real-Time Physics

Full rigid body dynamics with Cannon.js. Collision detection, gravity simulation. Physics-driven binding constraints and fragment behavior.

🎨

Material System

PBR materials with roughness and metalness. Procedural textures and patterns. Wood, Metal, Stone, Glass presets. Color tinting and normal maps.

💡

Professional Lighting

3-point studio lighting setup. Soft shadows with 2048×2048 resolution. ACESFilmic tone mapping. Atmospheric fog for depth.

📷

Camera Controls

OrbitControls for intuitive navigation. Flight mode with WASD controls. Camera presets for quick views. Smooth transitions.

🎲

Object Placement

6 primitive shapes: Sphere, Cube, Cylinder, Cone, Torus, Plane. Transform modes (move, rotate, scale). Possession mode for direct control.
